Why we are doing Altcampus?

AltCampus offers a 6-month, online programming bootcamp in Dharamshala, India, with a focus on recent college graduates, professionals looking to switch careers, and anyone who needs guidance in to learning to code.

The AltCampus full stack web development program covers HTML, CSS, Core JavaScript, React, Redux, NodeJs, and Databases. Students learn through hands-on problems and projects, pair programming, and one-on-one sessions with mentors.
